What is Clippy?

Clippy is a free online clipboard and web clipboard that lets you share text online and copy between devices through a simple URL. Think of it as a temporary clipboard in the cloud: paste your content, share the link, and anyone with the URL can access it instantly.

Unlike traditional clipboard tools, Clippy supports clipboard sharing with real-time synchronization, optional PIN protection, and instant text sharing without creating an account. It works as a secure clipboard for sensitive data when you enable encryption or PIN locks, and as a disposable clipboard that cleans itself up after expiry.

Whether you need to move a snippet between your phone and laptop or share files online for a few hours, Clippy is built for quick, ephemeral transfers — not long-term storage.
